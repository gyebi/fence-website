Most projects are delivered in 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. We always give you a fixed timeline before we start — no surprises.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. FenceX is built for non-technical founders. We translate your vision into a product — you focus on your business.
What happens after launch?
We don't disappear. You can choose a monthly maintenance plan for ongoing support, updates, and new features as your startup grows.
How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on your project scope. We offer startup-friendly packages across three tiers — Basic, Growth, and Scale. Book a free call and we'll give you a clear quote within 48 hours.
Which markets do you serve?
We serve startups across the United States, United Kingdom, and Africa — with a particular passion for African founders building for global markets.
